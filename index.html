Who We Are

About B.SMART

B.SMART is an industrial distribution and supply chain solutions company serving manufacturing enterprises across India with a comprehensive portfolio of industrial products and support services.

We consolidate multiple categories of industrial supply under one dependable partner — safety equipment, labelling and barcode systems, adhesives, fasteners, tools, electrical and automation components, pneumatic and hydraulic parts, packaging, and MRO consumables.

B.SMART also offers in-house 3D printing and rapid prototyping, extending our distribution expertise into custom manufacturing for parts, décor, signage and personalised gifting.
